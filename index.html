Welcome to SearchTek

Technology - People Consulting - Outsourcing.

Who are we ?

Searchtek specializes in the field of custom software development and human resource consulting. We believe in digital transformation in the industry and help clients adopt and embrace the changing world of technology. Our specialist recruiters and subject matter experts are committed to helping businesses hire the right talent at the right time. At all times we attempt to provide fewer but better-fit candidates.

About Us

Vision

Be the pre-eminent search firm unleashing the potential of people.

Mission

1. To match the needs of client's and candidates.
2. Facilitate discovery.
3. To partner on the client's talent journey.

Company Overview

We endeavor to deliver impactful ways of helping customers hire the right talent at the right time. Providing differentiated service, dedicated recruitment best practice adoption, and an unflinching commitment to clients and candidates.

Services

Retained Executive Search

Focus is on recruiting CXO and middle managers, Candidates who impart our goals, strategic planning, and decision making for the entire business.

Contingent Recruitment Offerings

The focus is on recruiting managerial and specialist positions. Functional experts who deal with people, customers, and projects. A second in line for CXO or aspiring leaders within the organization.

SME Deployment

The focus is to recruit a range of managerial, specialists and Staffing positions in the IT/ITE world.

Outsource Project Management

The focus is to fulfill software product and/or project outsourcing requirements. Turnkey solutions for technology outsourcing.

Software Development

The focus is on developing software to meet client's unique requirements. Our Software products are flexible, agile & tailor-made to meet your requirements.
